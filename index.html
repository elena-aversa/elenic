Ciao! This is Elena Aversa. Communication designer, PhD student in design, PhD students' representative.

Doing stuff with DensityDesign Lab, Politecnico di Milano.

Currently teaching Data Visualisation at Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti (NABA), Università degli Studi di Udine, POLIMI Graduate School of Management, and occasionally offering training courses for companies.

Researching on the role of information visualisations in the circulation of information disorders on social media platforms during societal crisis situations
